# Common Indian Sign Language (ISL) Signs

## Overview

Indian Sign Language (ISL) is used by Deaf communities across India.
It uses hand gestures, facial expressions, and body movements to
communicate meanings.

---

# Hello

Meaning:
A greeting used when meeting someone.

Gesture:
Raise hand near forehead and move it outward.

---

# Thank You

Meaning:
Used to express gratitude.

Gesture:
Move fingertips from chin forward.

---

# Yes

Meaning:
Shows agreement or confirmation.

Gesture:
Move closed fist up and down.

---

# No

Meaning:
Shows disagreement or refusal.

Gesture:
Move fingers to indicate rejection.

---

# Please

Meaning:
Used for polite requests.

Gesture:
Move open palm in a circular motion on chest.

---

# Sorry

Meaning:
Used to apologize.

Gesture:
Make a fist and move it on the chest.

---

# Help

Meaning:
Requesting assistance.

Gesture:
Place one hand over another and lift upward.

---

# Love

Meaning:
Expression of affection.

Gesture:
Cross arms over chest like a hug.

---

# Eat

Meaning:
Refers to eating food.

Gesture:
Move fingers towards mouth.

---

# Water

Meaning:
Refers to drinking water.

Gesture:
Use W hand shape near mouth.

---

# Stop

Meaning:
Command to stop something.

Gesture:
Raise open palm facing forward.

---

# Good

Meaning:
Shows something positive.

Gesture:
Move open hand forward from chin.

---

# Bad

Meaning:
Shows something negative.

Gesture:
Move open hand downward from chin.
